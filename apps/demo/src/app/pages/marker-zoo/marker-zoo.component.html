Marker Zoo

What this proves

Every marker lives at the tree path it describes. The walker (materializeMarkers) walks the initial-state literal recursively and substitutes each marker at the exact location you placed it. No root-level withX(...) composition.

In @ngrx/signals, every with* feature (withState, withComputed, withMethods, withHooks, withProps) composes against the entire store. To get the equivalent of this page, you'd either flatten the state hierarchy or split into multiple stores. SignalTree lets you keep the domain shape and attach behavior where it semantically lives.
