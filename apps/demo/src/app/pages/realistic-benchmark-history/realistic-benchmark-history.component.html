üìà Why Real-World Benchmarks Matter

Synthetic benchmarks lie. Real-world usage patterns tell the truth. Most state management benchmarks test isolated operations (add item, update field). SignalTree's benchmarks use weighted frequency multipliers based on actual usage data from 40,000+ developers.

üéØ Weighted by Reality

Reads happen 100x more than writes. Selectors run constantly. Serialization is rare. Our benchmarks weight operations by real-world frequency, not 1:1 like traditional tests.

üî¨ Statistical Rigor

Every result includes reliability scores (60-100%), environment calibration, and P-values. Understand measurement confidence, not just raw numbers.

üåç Cross-Platform
