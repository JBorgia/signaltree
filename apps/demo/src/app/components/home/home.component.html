‚ùì What is SignalTree?

JSON branches, reactive leaves. SignalTree treats your state like a tree ‚Äî the branches are your JSON paths ($.user.profile), and the leaves are reactive signals you read and write.

No actions. No reducers. No selectors. You model state as data, not mechanics. Access feels obvious: tree.$.user.profile.name() ‚Äî fully type-safe, IDE-discoverable, with fine-grained reactivity layered transparently on top.

With perfect type inference at 15+ nesting levels and industry-leading 0.036ms performance, SignalTree makes complex state management simple, fast, and intuitive.
